Pwnagotchi

A Pwangotchi is a tool that utilizes bettercap and operates on a Raspberry Pi to monitor its surrounding Wi-Fi environment, aiming to capture crackable WPA key material. It collects this data by passively sniffing. The captured material is stored on disk as PCAP files, which include various handshakes supported by hashcat, such as full and half WPA handshakes and PMKIDs.
